My story

I am a highly motivated individual aiming at continously expanding my comfort zone. I have been doing it during my academic curriculum as well as in personal life, seeking diversity and internationality.

Academically, my path has developed from classical studies up until economic, and lastly Data sciences and AI. Since then I became a passionate programmer, with knowledge and hands-on experience in many different fields.

I will be graduating in June from a 2-year Master's, offered jointly by CentraleSupélec (Paris-Saclay) and ESSEC Business School in Paris (#3 in the world in the field for QS Ranking). I will be having two majors, one in Data Sciences and one in Business Analytics.

Until the end of studies, I will be working with Transvalor company in order to present solutions based on Graph NNs as alternatives to FEM in the heavy industry sector. Recent professional experiences comprise researcher positions with Médiamétrie and HEC Paris, as well as with the ESSEC department of finance.
